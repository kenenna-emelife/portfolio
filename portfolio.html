What was the 1st car? Image result for cars Benz Patent Motor Car On January 29
, 1886, Carl Benz applied for a patent for his “vehicle powered by a gas engine.”
The patent – number 37435 – may be regarded as the birth certificate of the
automobile. In July 1886 the newspapers reported on the first public outing of the
three-wheeled Benz Patent Motor Car, model no. 1.
